keybind abilities:

on power key hold,
a user will begin to summon a field.
they will be restricted to their knife
and if the user either gets off of the ground
or gets too close to another field,
charging will halt.

once generated,
the user may crouch within it to get 90% cloak.

the field also has two parts:
the core (the dark sphere in the center)
and the rest.
if opponents are within a field from a user
and the latter one is also one some of their owned fields,
And crouching,

the opponent will get stunned,
damaged,
they will be stripped of cloak
and drained of health back to the user.

Being at the center will make opponents glow red
otherwise they will glow yellow.
it is necessary to be at the center to finish enemies off

fields will die after some time.

if the user has more than one field installed
and they are close enough
By approaching the core of a field
and go from standing -> crouched,
the user will get teleported
to their nearest field from that location.

approaching the core WHILE crouched
will not automatically teleport the user.

dying will automatically erase all created fields.
which also get destroyed at the end of the round.
