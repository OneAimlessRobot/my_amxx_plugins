this hero has a custom model

Passive abilities:
Tmp (extra damage + stun +glow on first hit)
Frying pan (extra damage + stun + glow on first hit)

deploying the tmp takes away all recoil for some time
Hitting with the tmp or pan fills up xp reservoir (moralizing xp)
Shooting teamates with those same weapons while the reservoir is >0
gives them xp points

On death,
A play die will be thrown
And if the chance comes,
they will respawn.
There is a maximum number of times this can happen.
If the user kills themselves they will not respawn through ester
Each time they respawn,
they will get godmode for some time,
Along with a very bright white glow.

After some time,
godmode will get removed
and a row of three shockwaves will fire around the user.
and the glow will be removed.

They are now in "reborn mode".
in reborn mode,
On SPACE+ Crouch,
they will fly in the direction they aim at
with a trail flowing from behind them
to indicate how much health they have.

Shooting a reborn Ester user as they fly
will reflect some damage back at the attacker

And getting hit as they charge towards
will create an explosion

At the end of the round,
spare moralizing xp will get awarded back to the user and reset to 0.
