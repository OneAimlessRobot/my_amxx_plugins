keybind abilities, PT1:

- while NOT in super angry mode:
transform into a jet!
The controls for it are:
- JUMP to accelerate,
- CROUCH to brake,
- MOVELEFT and MOVERIGHT
    (A and D keys by default)
    to roll left and right, respectively
- LEFT and RIGHT
    (left and right arrow keys by default)
    to steer left and right, respectively
- USE
    (E key by default)
    to launch a sonar that scans enemies
    within a radius
    displaying them on the radar
    for friends and you to see
- ATTACK1
    (left mouse click by default)
    to fire the machine gun
- ATTACK2
    (Right mouse click by default)
    to drop a bomb.
    (Try not to get caught in its blast.
    it is VERY strong
    and may immediatly destroy your plane)
- ALT1
    (bind "key" +alt1 in console to use this)
    to fire a rocket.

- RELOAD
    (R key by default)
    to toggle the engine on and off.
    if on the ground,
    Fuel will be replenished until topped


Jetplanes can attack each other!

destroying it while riding will kill you!

Ramming into walls with excessive speed
will result in a very large explosion
It also destroys the plane
and therefore you.

pressing the power key while riding a plane will eject you!

power key must be held for a while to fully summon the plane

Spawning a jetplane has a 120 second cooldown.
